Have you ever been discourage to pursue something because of your age?

Dikeme Mutombo, Junior Don Santos, Alex Morgan,and Antonio Gates are all athletes who started late into their sports career! Mutombo did not begin playing competitive basketball until he became a student at Georgetown University. Don Santos first began participating in martial arts at the age of 21, yet went professional within a year and he has since become one of the top MMA fighters in the world. Morgan Morgan did not begin playing soccer until the age of 13, but she took to the game quickly and is now the premier striker for the USWNT. Finall, Gates was signed by the San Diego Chargers in 2003 after not playing one snap of college football, but he has gone on to make eight Pro Bowl appearances while in the NFL. As you can see many athletes succesful atheletes all started late into their sports career. You should never feel as if your not able to do something becuase if your age. If this isn't enough to motivate you then try reasearching more athletes to gain your confience on trying out a new sport no matter what age you are!
